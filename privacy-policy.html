DoseTrace Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Introduction

DoseTrace ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the DoseTrace mobile application.

Information We Collect

DoseTrace collects the following information that you voluntarily provide:

Account Information: Email address and password when you create an account.

Profile Information: Name, date of birth, country, weight, height, gender, and health goals that you optionally provide to personalize your experience.

Health Data: Medication and supplement protocols, dosing schedules, dose logs, vial tracking information, and biomarker data that you enter into the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the DoseTrace service, including:

Storing your protocols, dose logs, and health tracking data so you can access them across sessions. Sending you push notifications for dose reminders you have configured. Syncing your data across devices when you opt into cloud sync.

Data Storage

DoseTrace uses an offline-first architecture. Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using SQLite. If you create an account, your data is also synced to our cloud servers (powered by Supabase) for backup and multi-device access. Cloud data is encrypted in transit using TLS.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not display advertisements. Your health data is never shared with insurance companies, employers, or any other entity.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your authentication credentials are permanently removed. Anonymized, de-identified health data may be retained for aggregate research purposes to improve the app.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time through the app's Settings screen. You can delete your account entirely from within the app, which will remove your access and authentication credentials.

Children's Privacy

DoseTrace is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete that information.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. Authentication tokens are stored securely using device-level secure storage. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
