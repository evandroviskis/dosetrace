DoseTrace

The all-in-one companion app for hormone replacement therapy. Track doses, monitor bloodwork, optimize your protocol.

Features

Dose Logging

Log every injection, cream, or patch with date, time, compound, and dosage.

Bloodwork Tracking

Upload lab results as PDFs or enter values manually. Track biomarkers over time.

Protocol Management

Set up your full protocol with multiple compounds, frequencies, and dosages.

Serum Curves

Visualize estimated serum levels based on your schedule and compound half-lives.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

DoseTrace ("the App") is developed by Evandro G Santos. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

DoseTrace collects the following data to provide its core functionality:

Account information (email address) for authentication purposes. Health-related data you voluntarily enter, including dose logs, bloodwork values, and protocol details. This data is stored securely in our cloud database to enable syncing across devices.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the app's functionality: storing your dose history, displaying your bloodwork trends, and managing your protocols. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers provided by Supabase. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure authentication.

Third-Party Services

DoseTrace uses the following third-party services: Supabase for authentication and data storage, and RevenueCat for subscription management. Each service has its own privacy policy governing the data they process.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app's settings. Upon deletion, all your personal data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Children's Privacy

DoseTrace is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at jootaerre@gmail.com.
